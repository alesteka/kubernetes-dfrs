GDPR Compliance Policy

For any inquiries or concerns regarding data protection and GDPR compliance, you may contact our designated Data Protection Officer at admin@dfrs.com

Introduction
This GDPR Compliance Policy outlines our commitment to protecting the privacy and rights of individuals who interact with our website. We are dedicated to complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and ensuring the secure handling of personal data.
Data Collection and Use
We collect and process personal data only for legitimate purposes and with the explicit consent of individuals. Personal data may include but is not limited to names, email addresses, and any other data provided voluntarily through our website forms or interactions.
Lawful Basis for Processing
We process personal data based on one or more lawful bases as defined by the GDPR. These may include the necessity of processing for the performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, consent from the data subject, or legitimate interests pursued by our organisation.
Data Subject Rights
We respect and uphold the rights of data subjects as outlined in the GDPR. Individuals have the right to request deletion, restrict processing, object to processing, and receive a copy of their data in a commonly used format.
Data Retention and Deletion
We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws and regulations. Upon request, we will promptly delete or anonymise personal data, ensuring that it is no longer identifiable or recoverable.To request data deletion head over to the request page: Data Deletion Request Page
Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of personal data. These measures include encryption, access controls, regular security audits, and employee training on data protection best practices.
Third-Party Disclosure
We do not share personal data with third parties without obtaining explicit consent, unless required by law or for the performance of contracted services. When sharing personal data with third parties, we ensure that appropriate data processing agreements are in place to safeguard the privacy and security of the data.
Cookies and Tracking Technologies
Our website may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and collect non-personal information. Users have the option to accept or decline cookies through their browser settings.
Data Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to individuals' rights and freedoms, we will promptly notify the affected individuals and the relevant supervisory authority in accordance with the GDPR's data breach notification requirements.
